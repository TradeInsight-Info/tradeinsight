TradeInsight surfaces what U.S. Congress members, corporate insiders, and the White House are trading — in real time. Two products:

- **Notifications** ($10/mo, free trial) — email or webhook alerts when a tracked trade is filed.
- **Data API** (free beta) — programmatic access to the same data, with historical coverage back to 2012.

## What's in this guide

1. [Installation](/docs/installation) — sign up and get an API key.
2. [Your first query](/docs/first-query) — pull a politician's recent trades in five lines.
