TradeInsight surfaces what U.S. Congress members and corporate insiders are trading — in real time. The Data API gives you programmatic access to all of it.

## What's available

- **Congressional trades** — every House and Senate disclosure since 2012, continuously updated as filings land.
- **Corporate insider trades** — SEC Form 4 filings (executive/director purchases and sales).
- **OHLCV price history** — 30y+ of daily US equity data, 7,000+ tickers, splits + dividends adjusted.
- **Market tickers** — company reference data with end-of-day price, % change, and market cap.
- **MCP server** — native Model Context Protocol for AI agents (Claude Code, Cursor, etc.).

## Tiers

| Tier | Access | History depth |
|------|--------|---------------|
| Free (beta) | 800 req/min | Last 12 months (congress/insider), 1 year (OHLCV) |
| Paid | Higher limits | Full history |
