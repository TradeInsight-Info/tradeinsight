Timely Alerts notify you the moment a U.S. Congress member or corporate insider
files a trade disclosure — so you see the signal while it still matters.

## Why track these trades

You can track trades from politicians and insiders and keep an eye on
particularly large purchasing. This can help empower you to potentially mirror
their success in the market. As Peter Lynch, the author of *One Up on Wall
Street*, said,

> Insiders might sell their shares for any number of reasons, but they buy them
> for only one: they think the price will rise.

Our service is designed to help you and make it possible to trade like Nancy
Pelosi, Congress members, or other insiders from different corporates.

Our core service is the notification of trades of politicians and insiders — it
brings you timely notifications to help you make better investment decisions.

## How it works

- We watch House, Senate, and SEC Form 4 filings continuously.
- When a new disclosure lands, matching subscribers get an alert.
- Pro plans add corporate-insider alerts, CSV export, and AI summaries.
