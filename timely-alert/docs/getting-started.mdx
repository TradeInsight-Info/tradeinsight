Timely Alerts notify you the moment a U.S. Congress member or corporate insider
files a trade disclosure — so you see the signal while it still matters.

## How it works

- We watch House, Senate, and SEC Form 4 filings continuously.
- When a new disclosure lands, matching subscribers get an alert.
- Pro plans add corporate-insider alerts, CSV export, and AI summaries.

Manage your alert preferences from your TradeInsight dashboard.
