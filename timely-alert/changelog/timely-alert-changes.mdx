date: 2025-10-26
description: A timeline of the congress and insider trade alert service, summarized from our product updates.

#### 2025-10-26

- Export any data table to CSV directly from TradeInsight.info.
- Japanese and Chinese language support across the platform.

#### 2025-10-20

- Broader LLM/AI integrations, including an AI Summary tab for recent trade insights.

#### 2025-10-01

- Launched AI Summaries on BlueSky — concise, AI-generated recaps of congressional trades. No user data is shared with LLM providers.

#### 2025-04-25

- Corporate insider (SEC Form 4) trade alerts for Pro users, at no extra cost.
- Insider alerts sent before the open and midday (US Eastern), capped at two per day to keep inboxes useful.

#### 2024-12-20

- Rebranded from PelosiTrade.com to TradeInsight.info, broadening beyond politician trades.
- Began integrating corporate insider data — groundwork for combining congressional and insider signals.

#### 2024-05-02

- Expanded coverage from Nancy Pelosi to 200+ members of Congress.
- Introduced the Pro plan to fund continued development; launched on Product Hunt.

#### 2023-11-01

- Launched as PelosiTrade.com — email alerts on Nancy Pelosi's congressional trades.
