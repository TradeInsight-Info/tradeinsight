We're excited to roll out several updates focused on productivity and analysis quality.
These include broader **LLM/AI integrations** and new **options tools** to help option traders calculate implied volatility and analyze profit/loss across strategies.

---

## What's New

- **More LLM + AI integrations**
  - We now have an [AI Summary tab](/dashboard/summary) on the left that you can use to quickly learn about recent trade insights.
- **Options tooling improvements**
  - Implied Volatility (IV) calculations with more robust inputs
  - Strategy P/L analysis and visualization across expiries and strikes
