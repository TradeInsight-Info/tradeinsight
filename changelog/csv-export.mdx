## Export Data Table to CSV

We are excited to announce a new feature that allows users to export data tables as CSV files directly from the TradeInsight.info platform.
This enhancement aims to provide users with greater flexibility in managing and analyzing their data.

![CSV Export Button](/static/2025/export-as-csv-Congress-Trade-TradeInsight.info.webp)

As you can see in the picture above, a new "Export CSV" button has been added to the data table,
this allow users to easily download the displayed data in CSV format with just a click of a button.

## Something More
> Japanese and Chinese Language Support

In addition to the CSV export feature, we are thrilled to announce that TradeInsight.info now supports Japanese and Chinese languages.
This expansion of language options is part of our commitment to making our platform accessible to a broader audience in the global community.

Now you can switch to Japanese or Chinese language to navigate and utilize the platform in your own preference.

![Language Selection Dropdown](/static/2025/language-selection-dropdown-TradeInsight.info.webp)
