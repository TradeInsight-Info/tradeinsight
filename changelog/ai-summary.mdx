As promised in our roadmap, we've integrated our service with **BlueSky**, one of the most popular microblogging platforms today.

We chose BlueSky as our starting point because of its developer-friendly ecosystem. On this platform, we've launched a new **AI Summary feature** that posts concise, AI-generated summaries directly to BlueSky.

This makes it easy for users to gain quick insights at a glance. Importantly, we do **not** share any of our users' information with the integrated LLM providers.

---

## Limits

While this feature is useful, please keep the following in mind:

- **Summaries only:** For full details, visit our [Latest Trade Page](/trade/latest).
- **Slight delay:** Like other summary features, there's a processing delay. For real-time updates, consider our [Pro Plan](/pricing).
- **LLM limitations:** Large Language Models may have occasional inaccuracies or formatting issues, so please review summaries with due diligence.
