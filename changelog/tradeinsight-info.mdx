Before Christmas 2024, we moved to a new domain to reflect our broader vision: to provide more than just politician insights. Our goal is to provide users with a broader set of market transparency tools.

While the user interface remained largely unchanged, we focused our efforts behind the scenes - integrating publicly available insider trading data into our system. This marked the beginning of our efforts to combine congressional trade alerts with corporate insider trading insights, laying the groundwork for a more comprehensive platform.

This evolution is part of our mission to provide users with timely, actionable information from multiple influential sources - and we're just getting started.
