We've expanded our service beyond Nancy Pelosi - users can now subscribe to email alerts for trades made by over 200 members of Congress. To support the continued development and operation of the platform, we've launched a Pro pricing plan.

This marks our second major milestone: expanding coverage to over 200 members of Congress. With this growth, we've moved away from our original free-only model. The Pro plan allows users to track the trading activity of all covered members.

We also recently launched on Product Hunt, which has brought a wave of new attention and valuable feedback from the community. We're incredibly grateful for the support and excited to see what's next.
