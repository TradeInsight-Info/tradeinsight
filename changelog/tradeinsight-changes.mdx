##### Jun 2026 — Data API & MCP

**Site rebuild + Data API beta**
Rebuilt tradeinsight.info from the ground up on Remix + Cloudflare Workers. Launched the Data API in free beta — REST endpoints for congressional trades, corporate insider trades, OHLCV price history, and market tickers. Native MCP server for AI agents (Claude Code, Cursor).

##### Timely Alerting Service

**Oct 2025 — CSV export, multi-language, options tools**
Export data tables as CSV. Japanese and Chinese language support. New options tools for implied volatility and P/L analysis. Broader LLM/AI integrations.

**Oct 2025 — AI Summary on BlueSky**
Integrated with BlueSky to post concise AI-generated summaries of congressional trades directly to the platform.

**Apr 2025 — Corporate insider trade alerts**
Corporate insider trading alerts now available to all Pro Plan users at no extra cost. SEC Form 4 filings delivered via email — up to 2 alerts daily before market open and midday.

**Dec 2024 — PelosiTrade becomes TradeInsight.info**
Moved to a new domain to reflect broader vision — not just politician insights, but wider market transparency tools. Began integrating corporate insider trading data.

**May 2024 — Subscribe to 200+ Congress members**
Expanded beyond Nancy Pelosi to cover 200+ members of Congress. Launched Pro pricing plan. Launched on Product Hunt.

**Nov 2023 — Launch**
Built a service for email alerts on Nancy Pelosi's congressional trades. Launched as PelosiTrade.com.
