The Insight Dashboard is **free**. Create an account and explore congressional
and corporate-insider trading activity online — no subscription required.

## What's included in the free plan

- Browse and filter congress and insider trades by name, ticker, source, and
  reported trade-size range.
- Explore the traders leaderboard and individual politician or insider profiles.
- Build a watchlist of the tickers you care about.
- Follow recent disclosure activity as new filings land.

The free plan covers everything you can *see* online. To have new disclosures
delivered to your inbox, add [Timely Alert](/docs/timely-alert).

## What you can see on the dashboard

- **Dashboard** — an at-a-glance overview of the latest congressional and
  insider trades.
- **Insight Activity** — a running feed of newly filed disclosures.
- **Traders** — a leaderboard of the most active politicians and insiders,
  with per-trader history.
- **Watchlist** — activity filtered to the tickers you follow.

Sign in at [app.tradeinsight.info](https://app.tradeinsight.info) to get started.
